About This Application

This AI-powered application helps identify medicinal plants from images. Upload a clear photo of a plant leaf or flower, and our machine learning model will analyze it to provide identification results.

Supported Plants: AMLA, BASIL, BLACK PEPPER, CHAMOMILE, CORIANDER, CUMIN, FENNEL, GINGER, LAVENDER, NEEM, OREGANO, PEPPERMINT, ROSEMARY, SAGE, THYME, TULASI, and many more.
